# Mechanism Memo

Mechanism memos define why a candidate might work before discovery begins.

Reviewable responsibilities:

- mechanism hypothesis stated before discovery metrics are reviewed
- plausible data sources and source-quality risks
- expected failure modes such as crowding, decay, leakage, sample selection, or cost sensitivity
- negative controls, ablation expectations, and robustness checks
- mapping from mechanism claim to feature, label, validation, replay, and closure evidence
