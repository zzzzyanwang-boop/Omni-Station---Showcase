# Alpha Research

Alpha and factor research is handled as an evidence-admission workflow.

Reviewable responsibilities:

- hypothesis registration
- factor profiling
- coverage and stability checks
- Rank IC / ICIR style summaries
- redundancy and correlation checks
- leakage sentinel results
- claim admission or block decision

Review surface:

- formulas and production metrics are represented through sanitized capability boundaries, manifests, and gate semantics rather than executable logic
