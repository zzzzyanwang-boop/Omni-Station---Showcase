# Layer 5 - Research Governance & Operations

Layer 5 defines how research is initiated, owned, reviewed, frozen, continued, stopped, waived, retired, and remembered.

Review focus:

- WorkOrder authority and route ownership
- current stage and allowed next action
- blocker state and review-board state
- freeze, stop, waiver, retirement, and closure decisions
- Research Mission Control and operator-visible state

Representative nodes:

- `work-orders/`
- `route-authority/`
- `research-mission-control/`
- `review-decisions/`
- `ops/`
- `ui/`
