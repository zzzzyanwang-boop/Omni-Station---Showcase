# Research Mission Control

Research Mission Control is the operator-facing view of active research lines.

Reviewable responsibilities:

- active route status with owner, stage, authority, and latest gate state
- current stage and blocker summary for missing evidence, failed gate, stale artifact, or waiting review
- waiting states for automation, reviewer decision, operator action, waiver, retirement, or closure
- next permitted action derived from route authority rather than free-form operator choice
- evidence and closure state projections linked to manifests and evidence envelopes
