## Reading Signal

The showcase should be read as architecture evidence, not as a runnable system. The important signal is the combination of:

- official Research OS layer names in `docs/architecture/five-layer-architecture.md`
- real public-safe file paths in `source/`
- sanitized capability placeholders in `redacted_capabilities/`
- architecture and flow documents under `docs/`
- toy examples under `examples/`
- pseudocode under `pseudocode/`

Together these show the system's engineering scope while preserving research confidentiality.
