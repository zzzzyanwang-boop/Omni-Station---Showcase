## Reading Signal

The repository should be read as architecture evidence, not as a runnable system. The important signal is the combination of:

- official Research OS layer names in `docs/architecture/five-layer-architecture.md`
- retained Python, TypeScript, and Rust module paths in `source/`
- sanitized capability placeholders in `redacted_capabilities/`
- architecture and flow documents under `docs/`
- synthetic contract fixtures under `examples/`
- pseudocode under `pseudocode/`

Together these show the system's engineering scope while keeping implementation and runtime material outside the review surface.
